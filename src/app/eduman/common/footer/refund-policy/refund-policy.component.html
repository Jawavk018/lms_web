Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund Policy:

  1. Once a course is purchased, refunds are only permitted under the following circumstances:
    • If misplaced the course order and notifies us within 24 hours of purchase.
    • If requests a refund within 24 hours of purchase and has not consumed any content from the course.
  2. Refunds will not be provided for courses that have been accessed, consumed, or downloaded.
  3. All refund requests must be made by contacting our support team at www.accoladeknowledge.com with the order details.

Cancellation Policy:

  1. Option to cancel their course order within 24 hours of purchase.
  2. Cancellation requests must be submitted by contacting our support team at www.accoladeknowledge.com
  3. Cancellations are only valid if not consumed any content, or downloaded from the course.
  4. If a cancellation request is received after 24 hours or after content consumption, the order will not be eligible for cancellation.
